Work & Case Studies

These projects focus on how I design analytics systems, structure data models, and turn business questions into reliable decision tools.

Revenue Intelligence System

Problem

Sales data was fragmented across different files and there was no clear, consistent view of performance, growth, or funnel health.

Approach

  • Designed a unified data model combining all sales sources
  • Defined revenue, growth, and funnel KPIs
  • Built executive and team-level dashboards in Power BI

What the system includes

  • Revenue trends and breakdowns
  • Funnel and conversion analysis
  • Performance by product, region, and team

Outcome

Reporting became faster, more consistent, and management could clearly see what was driving growth and where performance was leaking.

Financial Control System

Problem

There was no clear visibility into costs, no budget control, and no reliable way to track profitability across departments.

Approach

  • Built a financial data model with cost centers and categories
  • Designed P&L, budget vs actual, and variance analysis views
  • Created monthly financial control dashboards

What the system includes

  • P&L and cost structure analysis
  • Department spending tracking
  • Budget vs actual and variance analysis

Outcome

The cost structure became transparent and decision-makers could identify inefficiencies, risks, and opportunities for better control.

Inventory Intelligence System

Problem

The business was facing stockouts and overstocking, with no clear view of demand, stock health, or inventory movement.

Approach

  • Designed a stock and demand data model
  • Defined stock coverage, turnover, and movement KPIs
  • Built operations and planning dashboards

What the system includes

  • Stock coverage and turnover metrics
  • Slow-moving and fast-moving items analysis
  • Reorder and planning indicators

Outcome

Inventory decisions became data-driven and operations gained much better control over stock levels and planning.
